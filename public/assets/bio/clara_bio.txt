Clara Ungaro is a trained dancer skilled in contemporary, ballet, jazz, musical theater, hip hop, and improvisation. She has trained with Krista Miller, Christina Grady, Lacey Schwimmer, Alexis Gilbert, Jessica Richens, Lonni Olson, and more. She has received acting training from Gary Imhoff, Kelly Meyersfield, Adam Tait, Finola Hughs, and Zak Barnett. She has also received vocal training from Crystal Barron and Sarah Sandvig. Clara is also an aspiring choreographer and movement director. She has directed and choreographed several passion projects and choreographed emerging artists' music videos. She is a driven and passionate young artist who brings kindness to every professional setting.
